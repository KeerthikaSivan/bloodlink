About BloodLink

BloodLink is a life-saving digital initiative that connects generous blood donors with seekers in urgent need—across cities, hospitals, and individuals—through technology and compassion.

Our Donors

Donors are the heart of BloodLink. Healthy individuals who meet the eligibility criteria can register, check their status using our built-in predictor, and instantly respond to blood requests in their region.

Our Seekers

Patients, families, or hospitals in urgent need of blood can search based on blood group and city. The platform filters active, nearby donors and provides seamless communication to save critical minutes.

Prediction System

BloodLink’s smart prediction feature checks your eligibility in real time using criteria like age, weight, hemoglobin level, and donation history. This ensures safe donation practices and better health tracking.

Site Map Navigation

The website includes an easy-to-use navigation bar that links to all critical features: Donor page, Seeker page, Prediction form, Help center, and About Us. Whether you're giving or searching, everything is one click away.

Our Mission

At BloodLink, our mission is simple—save lives by connecting the right people at the right time. We use AI, data, and community power to make blood donation a proactive and rewarding act of kindness.
